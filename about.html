Who We Are

We are Jerusalem T&T — an award-winning creative experience creator in Israel, Jordan and Palestine. A team of travel designers who are in the know, and passionate about every detail.

Your time is precious. We put ourselves in your shoes as we plan each experience — so your clients feel at home away from home during their entire time with us.

We know the significance of every journey we create. Allow us to make each visit one that will be pleasantly engraved in your mind, heart and soul.

Our Purpose

Our Role Is…

To make sure your clients miss no detail and waste not a second. We'll show parts and corners of Israel, Jordan and Palestine that you never knew existed.

Our destinations are rich in History, Culture, Beauty, Nature, Religion, Architecture, Agriculture, Technology, and Wellness. We'll build you the itinerary of a lifetime.

We love Jerusalem and Tel Aviv, Haifa and Nazareth, Amman and Petra, Bethlehem and Jericho — north and south, east and west, and every grain of sand in between.
